When filling out medical forms, how often have you guessed about medications, surgery dates, vaccinations or insurance information? Now you don’t have to. Now you’ll have all your basic medical information whenever you need it, and give your doctors the accurate information they need to provide the very best care. Shahjalal University of Science and Technology
